FAQ

  • Can we cancel order from Medlife?

    The Customer may cancel the Order up to the point in time before Medilife has confirmed its acceptance of the Order and the Customer will not incur any cost in doing so. If the Customer wishes to vary its Order, the Customer needs to contact with admin to do so.

  • How long does it take for Medilife delivery?

    Hi dear, please be informed that our delivery lead time will takes six (6) to ten (10) working days (WM) and eight (8) to twelve (12) working days (EM) for processing starting from the date of order acceptance/confirmation by our E-Store

  • How do I pay at Medlife?

    We accept Cash on Delivery/Online Banking

  • What is tracking ID number?

    It is a unique ID number or code assigned to a package or parcel.

  • How do I find my tracking number?

    Look for your tracking number in the shipping confirmation.To track your order, customer can find their tracking number at the "To receive" section Be aware that you won't receive a tracking number until the order has actually shipped.
